Who We Are

Velvet Memories was born from a passion for celebrating life's most meaningful moments. We believe every occasion — from a quiet birthday to a grand wedding — deserves to be wrapped in warmth, beauty, and the richest chocolate. Our journey began with a simple idea: that the art of gifting should feel as extraordinary as the memory it creates.

Our Craft

Every piece we create is handmade with intention. From our signature chocolate collections — each flavor a story in itself — to our bespoke setups that transform spaces into dreamscapes, we pour artistry into every detail. Our chocolatiers work with the finest Belgian couverture, while our decorators weave colour, light, and texture into unforgettable environments.

Our Promise

We don't just create products — we create feelings. The feeling of opening a box and seeing something beautiful. The feeling of a room that takes your breath away. The feeling of a gift that says more than words ever could. That is the Velvet Memories promise: to make every moment feel like it was always meant to be this special.

Passion

Every creation begins with a love for the craft and a desire to make moments unforgettable.

Precision

From tempering to presentation, every detail is carefully measured and beautifully executed.

Artistry

We see chocolate and decoration as a canvas, turning every event into a visual masterpiece.
